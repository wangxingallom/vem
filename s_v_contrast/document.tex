 $ \mu_+=0 $\\
 
 $ \mu_-=\cos^{2}x+\cos^2 (\frac{x}{2}+\frac{\sqrt{3}y}{2})+\cos^2(-\frac{x}{2}+\frac{\sqrt{3}y}{2})+\cos^{2}(-x)+\cos^2 (-\frac{x}{2}-\frac{\sqrt{3}y}{2})+\cos^2 (\frac{x}{2}-\frac{\sqrt{3}y}{2})$\\
